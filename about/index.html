About Joint Insights

Why?

It is the mission of Joint Insights to help people leverage different perspectives and new approaches to make sense of their situation and solve the challenges they are facing.

How?

Focus on next-level problems

Once you solve one problem, you expose the next one. Embrace that, and constantly seek to find out what the root issue is.

Write the dictionary

Always baseline understanding. Train and explain as necessary before applying a concept.

Work on problems, not symptoms

People are often likely discussing symptoms. If that’s all we work on, the problem doesn’t go away.

Nourish transparency

The brutal truth puts us on the same page, and is most effective on moving us together in the same direction.

Who?

Joint Insights is owned and operated by Rich Visotcky.

About Rich Visotcky: It is my mission to help people leverage different perspectives and new approaches to connect with what is truly important in their situation and solve the challenges they are facing. I love to connect people with the principles behind leaner, agile processes and practices so they build elastic skills and adaptable approaches to deal with evolving challenges.

I’m a seasoned agile trainer and coach, Professional Scrum Trainer with Scrum.org, one of two stewards for the Professional Scrum Foundations course, and Management 3.0 facilitator bringing years of experience in helping hundreds of people learn agile concepts and put them into practice in their daily work and lives.
